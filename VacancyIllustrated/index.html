3,868

The total amount of vacant land in Saint Louis is 3,868 acres or 15.65km2. That is the size of the entire Cocos Islands near Australia.

1,514

A total of 1,514 building floors are vacant in Saint Louis. If stacked one on top of the other, that would be more floors than 9 Burj Khalifas.

26,132

A total of 26,132 residential units are vacant in Saint Louis, which is more than four times the 6,179 estimated chronically homeless individuals in the entire state of Missouri.

Data Methods

  • Using existing OpenSTL vacant dataset from ~2017
  • Acreage is sum of Acres field.
  • Floors is sum of ComStories field.
  • Number of residential units come from the ResUnits field of STLVacancy's Vacant Properties Dataset (June 30, 2018).
  • Homelessness statistics come from United States Integracy Council on Homelessness
